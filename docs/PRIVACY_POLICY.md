# Recovery Companion — Privacy Policy (summary for stores & support)

**Last updated:** April 16, 2026

## No server-side Recovery Companion account

Recovery Companion is built around **on-device storage**. We do **not** register you for a separate Recovery Companion cloud login, hosted user profile, or central account database that we delete when you tap “Delete account.”

**“Delete account” in the app** means: we **permanently erase the data the app has stored locally** on your phone or tablet (see in-app Settings for the exact categories). That is the practical equivalent of account deletion for this product.

## What may still exist outside the app

- **Apple App Store / Google Play** may retain purchase, subscription, and billing records tied to your **store account**. Those are managed in the store’s subscription settings, not inside Recovery Companion’s servers (we do not operate that billing database).
- **Your device backups** (iCloud, Google backup, device-to-computer backup) may include app data until you remove the app or exclude it from backup, according to your platform settings.

## Data location

Recovery content and preferences you enter in the app are intended to stay **on your device** unless you explicitly use a feature that sends data elsewhere (if applicable in your build). Review the in-app descriptions for any optional or demo features you enable.

## Contact

Use the support channel listed on your app store listing for privacy questions or data practices requests.
